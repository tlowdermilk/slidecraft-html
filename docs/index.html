Focus

Current focus

I’m focused on customer-connected product innovation in the age of AI: how teams identify meaningful problems, co-create with customers, design usable solutions, and understand the human impact of new tools and workflows.

The work is part discovery, part design strategy, part technical sensemaking, and part organizational learning. I’m interested not only in what AI makes faster, but what it changes about collaboration, trust, quality, and the way teams learn.

Customer insight Interaction models Product storytelling Developer experience AI adoption Organizational learning

Point of view

A simple working belief

Great products come from teams that know how to learn. The tools change. The methods evolve. But the hard part remains deeply human: asking better questions, making sense of what we hear, and turning that learning into decisions that improve people’s lives.

Reduce the distance between product teams and the people they serve.
